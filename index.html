Sales Outreach Process

A comprehensive guide to turning businesses into clients. Follow these steps carefully to maximize your success rate.

1Research

• Research the company and team members thoroughly
• Analyze their social media presence
• Identify content types they currently use
• Determine if they have existing marketing/videography team
• Understand their main business focus
• Prepare specific talking points about their business

2Identify Decision Maker

• Target business owner or general manager
• Get specific appointment times, not just contact info
• Schedule concrete follow-up meetings
• Document all interactions and contact attempts
• Build rapport with key staff members

3Present Value

• Clearly explain our company's services
• Focus on monthly sales generation potential
• Demonstrate understanding of their specific needs
• Show how our system addresses their challenges
• Present relevant case studies and success stories

4Close and Follow-up

• Aim for immediate commitment
• Set specific start date during meeting
• Schedule next steps for content creation
• Establish clear timeline for implementation
• Plan first recording session
